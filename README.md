# RISKY_brain_computer_interface
Here is a list of resource online introducing minimally invasive brain computer inetrface technology that allows direct communication from human brain to an external electronic device.

Such technology may provide potential solution to certain people in need, but lack of regulation and misuing of which would enable a small minory to undermine human dignity at an unprecedented scale.

Whichever described in those links is certain technically possible, but whether those would or should become an essential part of life in the future could still be highly debatable.

# Social Engineering
By using minimally invasive BCI to directly send auditory signal to someone's auditory cortex, and using the same device to access an victim's sensory input and instant thought, someone (let's call them brain invader) with access to the communication endpoint is able to communicate with the victim whenever the brain invader wants. With access to the victim's thoughts and daily experience, brain invaders can leverage social engieering to construct a story, threatening, harassing or manipulating the victim.
